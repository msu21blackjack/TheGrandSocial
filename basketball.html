Welcome to Basketball at The Grand Social

About Basketball at The Grand Social

At The Grand Social, we offer an exciting, high-tech basketball experience. Our **basketball bays** are equipped with real-size hoops and cutting-edge technology to create thrilling shooting-based games. It's like a pop-a-shot, but with a twist!

These sports bays can be rented by the hour and are great for individuals or groups. Challenge your friends, track your shooting stats, and have a blast with our interactive games.

Food and drink service is available for groups, so you can enjoy a snack or refreshment while competing in your basketball challenge!

Basketball Bay Schedule and Pricing

Our basketball bays are available for **hourly rentals**. Whether you're looking to shoot some hoops casually or engage in a fun competition, our bays are ready for your next game!

We recommend booking in advance, especially for groups or during peak hours.
