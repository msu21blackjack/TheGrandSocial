Welcome to Pickleball at Varsity Club

About Pickleball

Pickleball is one of the fastest-growing sports in the U.S., combining elements of tennis, badminton, and ping pong. Played on a smaller court, it's a great sport for all ages and skill levels!

At Varsity Club, we offer both drop-in sessions and court rentals. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced player, our pickleball courts are perfect for your next match.

Pickleball Schedule

Drop-in play is available anytime during regular business hours, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

After 5:00 PM, we suggest reserving a court to ensure availability.
