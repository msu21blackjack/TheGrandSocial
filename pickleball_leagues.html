Join Our Pickleball Leagues

Our pickleball leagues run from **late spring to early fall**, with league play happening on **weeknights from 5:00 PM to 9:00 PM**.

Recreational Leagues

Recreational leagues are designed for those who want to play for fun and stay active. Games are held on **Mondays and Wednesdays** from **5:00 PM to 9:00 PM**.

Competitive Leagues

If you're looking for a more competitive experience, our competitive leagues are held on **Tuesdays and Thursdays** from **5:00 PM to 9:00 PM**. Test your skills against some of the best players in the area!

League Schedule

  • **Recreational Leagues**: Mondays & Wednesdays, 5:00 PM - 9:00 PM
  • **Competitive Leagues**: Tuesdays & Thursdays, 5:00 PM - 9:00 PM

How to Sign Up

Sign up today to reserve your spot! Whether you're forming a team or joining as an individual, we'd love to have you join us.
