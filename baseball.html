Welcome to Baseball at The Grand Social

About Baseball at The Grand Social

Step up to the plate at The Grand Social, where our **Baseball Bays** take the sport to the next level! Whether you're working on your pitching speed, testing your aim with target games, or just having fun, we've got something for every baseball lover.

Our high-tech baseball bays offer an array of fun and challenging games. Test your pitching arm by going head-to-head with speed challenges or aim for moving targets to knock them down, carnival-style. You can also challenge your friends to a **home run derby** or take part in a **strikeout challenge**—the choice is yours!

**Food and drink service** is available so you can grab a bite and relax between games. These bays are perfect for friends, families, or team events, and they're ideal for anyone wanting to improve their skills or just have a great time.

Baseball Bay Games

Get ready for a unique baseball experience! Here are some of the exciting games you can enjoy in our baseball bays:

  • Pitching Speed Challenge: How fast can you throw? Test your pitching speed and see if you can break the radar!
  • Knock Over Targets: Knock down moving targets with pinpoint precision. It’s like a carnival game with a baseball twist!
  • Home Run Derby: Step up to the plate and see how many home runs you can hit in a row! Can you beat the clock?
  • Strikeout Challenge: Test your accuracy and try to strike out as many virtual batters as you can within the time limit.
  • Batting Accuracy Challenge: Players aim for specific zones on the field to test their hitting precision—can you hit the sweet spot every time?

Whether you want to challenge yourself or just have a little fun, our baseball bays provide a thrilling experience every time. The technology tracks your stats, so you can compare your results and even compete with friends!
